Developed as a freelance project, this game integrates a reward-based system where users earn cryptocurrency through gameplay progression. Built on top of a template and customized for scalability, the game reached tens of thousands of downloads and achieved strong commercial success.

**Technical Details**

* Engine: Unity
* Backend: Firebase (authentication & data tracking)
* Monetization: Reward-based system
* Integration: Crypto reward logic
* Deployment: Google Play Store

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your data or provide features.

The Application creates an anonymous user account automatically after onboarding. This account is used to store user-specific study data and enable core functionality such as study tracking and notifications.

The Application uses Firebase Authentication (anonymous sign-in) and Firebase services to manage user data securely.

Device push notification tokens are collected solely for delivering study reminders and session notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging.

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information and service-related notifications.

For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to Name. This information is stored and used as described in this privacy policy.

Child Safety Standards for LOV - Tracker

Last Updated: April 13, 2026
1 Zero Tolerance Policy

LOV - Tracker has a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any content that promotes the sexual exploitation of children (CSAE). We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and strictly adhere to international safety standards.

2 Reporting Mechanisms

We provide easy-to-use in-app reporting tools. Users can report any suspicious activity or inappropriate content immediately. Every report related to child safety is prioritized and reviewed by our moderation team within 24 hours.

3 Content Moderation and Monitoring

LOV - Tracker employs automated filters and manual review processes to detect and remove any harmful content. If we identify any material involving the exploitation of minors, the associated account will be permanently banned.

4 Legal Compliance and Reporting to Authorities

In accordance with regional and national laws, LOV - Tracker fulfills its legal obligations to report any instances of CSAM/CSAE to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and local law enforcement agencies.
